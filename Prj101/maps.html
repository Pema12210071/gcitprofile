COLLEGE MAP

Gyalpozhing College of Information and Technology is located in the Mongar District of Bhutan along the national highway between Mongar and Pemagatshel. From the Lingmethang it is located 7 km away and from the mongar town it is located 30 km away from it. It is located near the Kurichu hydropower project and the college campus has the area of 21 acres of land with one archery ground, one football ground, one basket ball court and one volley ball court.
